OUR PACKAGE

Cafe Delight Package

Introducing our Café Combo: Indulge in the perfect pairing of our signature coffee and a savory sandwich or a sweet pastry. Whether you're craving a quick bite or a leisurely treat, our combo offers a delicious blend of flavors to satisfy your cravings.

Couple Package

Introducing our Café Couple's Combo: Enjoy our signature coffee plus two treats of your choice. Perfect for sharing a cozy moment together.

Family Package

Experience our Family Café Combo, including four cups of freshly brewed signature coffee, four flaky croissants, four assorted muffins, two classic ham and cheese sandwiches, two turkey and avocado wraps, one decadent chocolate cake, and one fruit tart—a feast for sharing and enjoying together.

Romantic Dinner

Experience our Romantic Dinner Combo, featuring two glasses of house wine, two specialty cocktails, a shared cheese and charcuterie platter, two filet mignon steaks with mashed potatoes and vegetables, and a chocolate fondue for two, ensuring an intimate and unforgettable dining experience.

Cande Light Dinner

Step into romance with our Candlelight Dinner Combo: Enjoy two glasses of fine wine, share a platter of artisanal cheeses and charcuterie, savor two perfectly cooked filet mignon steaks with sides, and end with a decadent chocolate fondue for two—a truly memorable dining experience awaits.

Bulup To Go

Introducing our Bulup Café To-Go Combo: Dive into a convenient and delicious experience with a curated selection of our finest offerings, including a choice of one signature beverage, such as our specialty coffee or refreshing iced tea, accompanied by a freshly baked pastry or sandwich of your choice. It's the perfect grab-and-go option, ensuring you can enjoy the flavors of Bulup Café wherever your day takes you.
